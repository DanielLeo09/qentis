How Qentis works

Three steps. One blockchain record. Zero forgery.

1
Issuer registers

An approved institution registers an item. Qentis generates a cryptographic hash and writes it permanently to the blockchain.

2
QR code attached

The issuer receives a QR code, serial number, and optional digital signature or watermark to attach to the item.

3
Anyone verifies instantly

Any verifier scans, types, or photographs the item. Qentis queries the blockchain and returns an AUTHENTIC result in under 3 seconds.
